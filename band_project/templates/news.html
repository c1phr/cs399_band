Duke Ayers - Master Piece Sold!

Early this morning, Duke sold his first master piece for over $1,000,000! He was quoted as saying "I can't believe that I finally have sold my very first piece of art... I mean, it took so much time and effort to make it and now that I have sold it I am contemplating what I will be doing with my next piece of art or the direction that of which I should be headed with the newest design. I should seriously become pro." We wish his future career the best of luck!
